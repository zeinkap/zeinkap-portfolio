Who is He?

QA tester and Web Developer

A developer by hobby and a tester by profession. I am passionate about building and designing web applcations. It all started on the eve of December, 22nd, 2016. I enrolled in an introductory course to HTML & CSS produced by codecademy. I freshly remember typing my first line of code, "Hello World." Eight months later, I continue to self teach myself programming and in the process I have completed several courses covering topics such as: JavaScript, jQuery, bootstrap, SQL, selenium, command line and Git. I aim to perfect and expand my skill set.
